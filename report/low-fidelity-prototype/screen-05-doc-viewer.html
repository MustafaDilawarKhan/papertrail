Low-fi design notes

  • Two-pane layout: PDF on the left, AI chat anchored on the right. The single most important decision and the one the survey validated most strongly (B4 = 4.67).
  • Multi-document tabs above the viewer; tab state persists in localStorage.
  • Citation cards inside each AI answer are clickable — they jump the PDF pane to the exact page + bounding box (the yellow block in §2 illustrates the resulting highlight).
  • Toolbar is right above the PDF (not inside the chat) so annotation tools feel "attached" to the document.
  • On screens < 1024 px, the chat pane collapses into a slide-over drawer (see Screen 5b in the responsive section).
