Low-fi design notes

  • Three view modes (Editor / Preview / LaTeX) toggled in the top bar. “Preview” hides editor chrome; “LaTeX” shows the generated IEEEtran source.
  • Block-based palette on the left; drag-and-drop into the canvas.
  • Outline rail on the right doubles as a structure inspector — properties of the selected block appear below.
  • Equations rendered via KaTeX; figures and tables are atomic blocks that don't break across columns.
  • Auto-save indicator next to the document title (cosmetic in the prototype, real in the high-fi build).
